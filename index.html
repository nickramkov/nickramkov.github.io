Lamborghini URUS

THE LAMBORGHINI URUS IS A HIGH PERFORMANCE LUXURY SUV MANUFACTURED BY ITALIAN AUTOMOBILE MANUFACTURER LAMBORGHINI

IT WAS UNVEILED ON 4 DECEMBER 2017 AND WAS PUT ON THE MARKET FOR THE 2018 MODEL YEAR

THE NAME COMES FROM THE URUS, THE ANCESTOR OF MODERN DOMESTIC CATTLE, ALSO KNOWN AS THE AUROCHS
